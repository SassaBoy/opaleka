Opaleka Payment Terms

Effective Date: February 2025 | Secure & Transparent Transactions

Accepted Payment Methods

Opaleka supports secure transactions using the following payment methods:

  • Credit & Debit Cards (Visa, MasterCard, Amex)
  • Mobile Money (E-wallets, PayGate)
  • Bank Transfers & EFT
  • Secure Payment Gateway (DPO Group)

Subscription & Billing

Service providers are required to subscribe to our platform using a 30-day subscription plan.

  • 30-Day Subscription: NAD 200

All subscriptions are non-refundable. Auto-renewal is enabled unless manually canceled before the renewal date.

Refund & Cancellation Policy

Payments for completed transactions are final. Refunds may only be issued under the following conditions:

  • Unauthorized transaction or duplicate charge.
  • Service not rendered due to system failure or verified provider non-compliance.

Refund requests must be submitted within 7 days of the transaction date.
